Exclusive Interview with Team India, Winners of the 2024 T20 World Cup

We had the opportunity to catch up with Team India's captain, Rohit Sharma, and other key players after their thrilling victory in the 2024 T20 World Cup. Here's what they had to say:

Rohit Sharma (Captain)

"It's an incredible feeling, winning the T20 World Cup for the second time. The team played exceptionally well throughout the tournament, and it's a testament to our hard work and dedication."

Virat Kohli

"I'm proud of the way the team performed, especially in the final match. We knew it wouldn't be easy, but we stuck to our plans and executed them well."

Key Takeaways from the Interview:

  • Team Effort: The players emphasized the importance of teamwork and collaboration in their success.
  • Preparation and Planning: The team credited their thorough preparation and planning for their victories in the tournament.
  • Leadership: Rohit Sharma's leadership was praised by the players, who appreciated his guidance and support throughout the tournament.
  • Future Goals: The team expressed their desire to continue performing well and winning tournaments, with a focus on the upcoming ODI World Cup.

Awards and Accolades:

  • Player of the Tournament: Jasprit Bumrah
  • Most Runs: Rohit Sharma (394 runs)
  • Most Wickets: Jasprit Bumrah (20 wickets)
