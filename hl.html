2024 T20 World Cup Match Highlights

The 2024 T20 World Cup has witnessed some thrilling matches, with Team India delivering impressive performances. Here are some of the notable highlights:

India vs Pakistan Group Stage Match

Suryakumar Yadav's thrilling 50-ball 92 runs, including 7 sixes and 4 fours, stole the show. Hardik Pandya's 3-wicket haul restricted Pakistan to 178/6. India successfully chased the target, winning by 5 wickets with 2 balls remaining.

India vs Australia Group Stage Match

Rohit Sharma's blistering 56-ball 115 runs, including 10 sixes and 7 fours, was a treat to watch. Jasprit Bumrah's 2-wicket haul helped restrict Australia to 204/7. India successfully chased the target, winning by 6 wickets with 4 balls remaining.

India vs South Africa Semi-Final Match

Virat Kohli's match-winning 76 runs off 48 balls, including 5 fours and 2 sixes, was instrumental in India's win. Suryakumar Yadav's 34-ball 61 runs set up India's total of 185/5. India's bowlers restricted South Africa to 179/8, winning by 6 runs.

India vs England Group Stage Match

Jasprit Bumrah's 5-wicket haul restricted England to 159/8. Rohit Sharma's 45-ball 62 runs helped India chase down the target. India successfully chased the target, winning by 7 wickets with 10 balls remaining.
