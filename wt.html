2024 T20 World Cup Winning Team: India

India emerged as the champions of the 2024 T20 World Cup, defeating South Africa in the final match by 7 runs. This victory marked India's second T20 World Cup title, with the first one being in 2007.

Key Players:

  • Rohit Sharma (Captain): Led the team with his exceptional batting and leadership skills.
  • Virat Kohli: Played a crucial role in the final match, scoring 76 runs off 48 balls.
  • Jasprit Bumrah: Took 5 wickets in the group stage match against England and was named the Player of the Tournament.
  • Suryakumar Yadav: Scored a thrilling 92 runs off 50 balls against Pakistan in the group stage.

Tournament Statistics:

  • Most Runs: Rohit Sharma (394 runs)
  • Most Wickets: Jasprit Bumrah (20 wickets)
  • Highest Score: Suryakumar Yadav (92 runs off 50 balls)
  • Best Bowling Figures: Jasprit Bumrah (5/20 against England)
